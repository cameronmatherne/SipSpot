SipSpot Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

SipSpot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Location — We request access to your device location while the app is in use. This is used solely to center the map near you and show nearby restaurants. We do not store or transmit your location to our servers.
  • Account information — If you create an account, we collect your email address. This is stored securely via Supabase and used only to authenticate you and remember your saved spots.
  • Saved spots & favorites — If you are signed in, the spots you bookmark are stored in our database so they sync across devices.
  • Problem reports — If you submit a report about a spot or the app, we store the message and (if signed in) your user ID to help us follow up.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not track your location in the background.
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Our backend database and authentication provider. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps — Used to render the in-app map. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

Account data and saved spots are retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us, which will remove all associated data from our systems.

Children's Privacy

SipSpot is intended for users 21 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@sipspot.app.
